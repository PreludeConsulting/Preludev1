## O*NET database

This package prepares files derived from the O*NET database by the U.S. Department of Labor, Employment and Training Administration (USDOL/ETA), used under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license.

Official landing page:
https://www.onetcenter.org/database.html

License:
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

O*NET® is a trademark of USDOL/ETA.
