# FAFSA and Federal Financial Aid Reference

## Source
- URL: <https://studentaid.gov/>
- Owner: Federal Student Aid, U.S. Department of Education

## Use for
- FAFSA basics and eligibility framing
- Federal grants, loans, and work-study
- Aid estimator references
- Official terminology and definitions
- Federal warnings, limits, and repayment framing

## Required answer behavior
1. For federal aid questions, remind students they must submit the FAFSA to be considered for federal aid.
2. Encourage users to verify deadlines and details on StudentAid.gov and school financial-aid pages.
3. Avoid claiming school-specific aid amounts unless verified from an official source.
4. Never invent grants, deadlines, loan terms, or eligibility requirements.

## Canonical reminder
"Complete the FAFSA and verify current deadlines/requirements on StudentAid.gov."
